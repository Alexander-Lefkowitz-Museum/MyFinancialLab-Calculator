Scenario

If John invests $1,850 today in an asset earning a 10% rate of return (compounded annually), how much will he have after two years?

Step 1

Press the RESET button to clear all the previous entries.

Step 2

Enter the present cash outflow (in this case 1850), and then press the +/- button followed by the PV button.

Step 3

Enter the periodic interest rate (in this case 10 percent), and then press the I/Y button.

Step 4

Enter the number of time periods (in this case 2), and then press the N button.

Step 5

Press the CPT button and then the FV button to solve for the future value of the lump sum.
